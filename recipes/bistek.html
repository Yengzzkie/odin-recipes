Description

Bistek, short for "Bistek Tagalog," is a classic Filipino dish known for its mouthwatering combination of savory and tangy flavors. It typically consists of thinly sliced beef marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, calamansi (or lemon) juice, garlic, and pepper. The marinated beef is then pan-fried and simmered in the marinade, creating a delectable sauce. Bistek is often garnished with saut√©ed onions for added sweetness and texture. This dish is a favorite in Filipino cuisine and is commonly served with rice.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce (Optional)
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound beef steak, sliced 1/4-inch thick
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided, or as needed
  • 4 potatoes, cut into wedges (Optional)
  • 1 large onion, cut into rings
  • 1/8 cup water (Optional)
